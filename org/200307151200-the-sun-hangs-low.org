#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: The sun hangs low

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The sun hangs low on the river Arno,
a goblet of fire over a lake of wine;
casting images of gold on panes above the knoll
of the bank and bridges where was an empire.
#+END_VERSE
