---
title: The Moth King
tags: poetry
location: Chocolate Iguana, Tucson, Arizona
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Behold!
I am the Moth Satan
wreathed in flame.

I glow with a heat
only lovers
can comprehend.

My scepter
is my burning limbs;
my crown,
my blazing brow;
my throne,
the torment
that surrounds me...

Behold!
I am the Moth Angel --
for fire and love
have made me king.
#+END_VERSE
