---
title: Behold the higher
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
If you claim
  to be a lover of diamonds,
yet your eye
  lingers on rocks and dust
it tells the lie.

Not while the heart
  holds envy
for lesser things
  has it beheld the higher.
#+END_VERSE
