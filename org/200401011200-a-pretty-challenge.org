title: A pretty challenge
tags: poetry
edited: 03/27/2005
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Whenever I look at the girls of the world,
every face is a pretty challenge.
Such beauty, beheld with my outward eyes;
will I ever see life, inwardly, that way?
#+END_VERSE
