#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Sweet girl

#+BEGIN_VERSE
O sweet girl!
Do you know that I was
solid when you met me
but now I flow along?

Love has that effect:
It gives even a mouse
the chance to fly!
or a mote of dust
to sparkle like the Sun...
#+END_VERSE
