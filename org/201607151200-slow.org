title: Slow
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
In a time languorous
the moments are honey
long, drawn-out seconds
pool around me
a slow, slow heat
pervades my being.
Yesterday, my other life.
is lost to memory.
The future calls in
mellifluous tones
falling, falling
a thousand years from now.
#+END_VERSE
