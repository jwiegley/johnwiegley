title: Flight
tags: poetry
edited: 03/17/2004
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
If a bird's wings are so soft
  that beat the air
and so small as to be tiny dots,
  far away,
then why do I feel them,
  brushing against my heart,
whenever I gaze up at the sky
  and join in spirit
  their vaulting, skyward flight?
#+END_VERSE
