---
title: Crumbs of camphor
tags: poetry
edited: 03/22/2004
location: Italy
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The lights of evening above,
crumbs of camphor
scattered on sackcloth;
or the leavings of a banquet in heaven
whose connosieurs
dine only on light.
#+END_VERSE
