title: I plead
tags: poetry
edited: 11/13/2003
location: Guest room in the Rhodes' house, 312 Palmer Ave
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
I plead to your beauty: end my pain.
My skin is a door
and your fingers, each a key.
This house, wherein heaven dwells,
awaits your soft caress...
Awaken me.
#+END_VERSE
