#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: The wave is powerless

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The wave is powerless
to step away from the ocean...
all his substance is borrowed.
It doesn't mean he doesn't move about,
play and crash,
only that he
has no existence
separate from the sea,
and should recognize this
when regarding it.
#+END_VERSE
