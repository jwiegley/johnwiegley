#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Without words

#+BEGIN_VERSE
It can all be said
in one word
if our hearts
share the vocabulary.

But even then,
it's not to be said --
better if shown --
the nights themselves
will tell the meaning.
#+END_VERSE
