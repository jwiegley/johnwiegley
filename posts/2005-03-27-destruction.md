---
title: Destruction
category: Poems
---

    If poetry is devastating,
    let me be destroyed.

    If love is painful,
    let me hurt.

    Just so,
    as the flames burn
    please care for my ashes.

    I may not know what I ask for --
    but I do know this:
      The life that I've lived so far
      is not how I want to die.
