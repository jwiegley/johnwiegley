#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: The melding

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Looking at a blank sheet
seeing every discourse
as if graven in strokes of light;
the Pen still upon the page
the Hand trailing tears --
wept from a Face
beyond all vision.
#+END_VERSE
