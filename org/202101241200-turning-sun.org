#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Turning sun

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Day after day,
student watches
the sun rise and set;
asks the master:

“How long has the Sun
turned round the Earth?”

Hears reply:
"Not yet."
#+END_VERSE
