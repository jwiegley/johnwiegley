#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: A young kitten

#+BEGIN_VERSE
I feel like a young kitten,
head on its mother's lap,
kneading gently and
taking in the milk of truth and reality,
until soon it's strong enough
to open new, curious eyes
and bear the light.
#+END_VERSE
