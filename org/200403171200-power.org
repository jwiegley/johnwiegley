---
title: Power
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Into the river I cast a stone
and the waters part immediately;
I feel like a conqueror: powerful.

Then eight thousand years go by.
The rock is gone; I am dust.
The meaning of power has changed.
#+END_VERSE
