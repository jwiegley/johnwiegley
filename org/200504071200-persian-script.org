#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Persian script

#+BEGIN_VERSE
As if my pen
had drunk too much
its joy beyond all keeping --
dancing across the page,
wild with meaning.

A slender reed
thrumming with songs
of passion and Persian princes;
these are not mere lines,
but the tracings of a pen
  turned whirling dervish.
#+END_VERSE
