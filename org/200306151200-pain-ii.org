title: Pain II
tags: poetry
edited: 11/30/2005
location: Spain
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
pain reaches up
fingers of nettles
a hand from a dark and bitter pool
deep within

as it wraps around
our bright, present moment
and begins to squeeze...

our mind is a flaming torch
held high in that hand
a tribute to the joys of tomorrow
#+END_VERSE
