---
title: The void
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The void
a pregnant nothing
poised upon the lips
but never spoken
hiding where eyes rest...
a glint of sun
a silent whisper
wherein all that is destroyed
finds life.
#+END_VERSE
