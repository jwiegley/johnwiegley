#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Borders

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The world is filled with borders --
or is it?

If the river runs dry,
and the riverbed fills up,
does that mean we're brothers again?

I forget what day you crossed it...
Apparently you've forgotten too.
#+END_VERSE
