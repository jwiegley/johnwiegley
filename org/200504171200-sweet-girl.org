title: Sweet girl
tags: poetry
edited: 04/25/2005
location: Horizon Park Center, Scottsdale, Arizona
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
O sweet girl!
Do you know that I was
solid when you met me
but now I flow along?

Love has that effect:
It gives even a mouse
the chance to fly!
or a mote of dust
to sparkle like the Sun...
#+END_VERSE
