---
title: Sudden flash
tags: poetry
location: 5130 (Barbara Swenson's house), Tucson, AZ
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Rainfall outside my window:
Water sweeps the sky.

A flash in the silence.

Painted on my mind's eye:
Images of distant peaks.

A sudden, dark rumble.
#+END_VERSE
