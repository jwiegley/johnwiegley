title: Silence
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Silence

allows me to think,
  to feel,
  to dream,
  to wonder,
  to realize,
  to awaken,
  to know.

I watch your eyes
play silently in the light --
what else is there?
#+END_VERSE
