#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Falling rain

#+BEGIN_VERSE
the drops of the rain
fall steadily down;
the grey clouds
the muted breeze
the scents
of a sodden earth.
the water soaks
into the ground's
own marrow.
the world is slowly
being dissolved
by a cleansing sky.
#+END_VERSE
