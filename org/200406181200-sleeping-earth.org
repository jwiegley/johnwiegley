---
title: Sleeping earth
tags: poetry
location: 380 Esplanade Ave #211
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Tonight, only peace.
The stars
wheel about
their constellations...
the sea
recounts
a hushed lullaby...
the moon
winks
at a sleeping earth...
and a herd of
silvered clouds
pass by.
#+END_VERSE
