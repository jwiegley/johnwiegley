title: Spider's Web
tags: poetry
edited: 03/22/2004
location: Italy
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The moon behind a spider's web,
making traceries of light;
only the eight-fingered darkness
by the side
to tell the truth of it.
#+END_VERSE
