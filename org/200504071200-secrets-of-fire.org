#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Secrets of fire

#+BEGIN_VERSE
How many a lover
  the flame of love has burned;
How many a knower
  laments what he had learned.

When fire teaches secrets
  the mind cannot withstand,
Show the flames your madness
  Behold: they understand.
#+END_VERSE
