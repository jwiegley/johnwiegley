---
title: Crucible of the spirit
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
If life is a crucible
of the spirit,
pride should be taken,
not in that fortune
excluded you from suffering,
but that you learned from it --
or had the fortitude to survive.
#+END_VERSE
