---
title: Life is a gift
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Your life is a gift.
Open it.
Learn who you are,
who you were made to be,
the fullness of your potential.
The Prophets came
to guide us in these things,
so I would say
that what God desires
is for us
to fully be.
#+END_VERSE
