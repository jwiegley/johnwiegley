#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Night, tonight

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Night, tonight
  fair black on
    drops of starling white;
a dreamer's sea
  for finer sails
    O captains of the light!
onward ho,
  to heaven's hold
    where angels take their flight
and join the
  twirl of galaxies
    who dance upon such heights.
#+END_VERSE
