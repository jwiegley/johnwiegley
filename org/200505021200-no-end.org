#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: No end

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Sometimes I feel such mysteries
moving through this pen!
telling the same truths
over and over again,
all of them, simple verses --
recollections of the Friend --
but though I write them down
there seems to be no end.
#+END_VERSE
