title: Sea
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The sea has its home
in the souls of those who remember;
never leaving them,
mingled with their blood,
awaiting the day when its many pilgrims
hearken to their heart's thirsty cry.
#+END_VERSE
