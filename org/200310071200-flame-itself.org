---
title: Flame itself
tags: poetry
edited: 11/07/2003
location: Cafe Torrefazione Italiano
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
If I were flame itself
and all the seas heaped upon me;
yet, undimmed, a spark would remain
to show my Love that I had been.
#+END_VERSE
