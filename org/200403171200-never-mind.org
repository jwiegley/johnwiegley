#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Never mind

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Never mind that I just ignored you,
  just passed you by,
  just voted to put you down,
  just kicked you,
  just ignored the fact of your being --

My parents made sure the word
"stranger"
allowed for anything.
#+END_VERSE
