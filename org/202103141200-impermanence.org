#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Impermanence

#+BEGIN_VERSE
All my life I've built
but castles of sand
Whether in my mind
or with my hands
Until destiny's wave
shall break upon the shore
And in silent surrender
whisper, "nothing more"
#+END_VERSE
