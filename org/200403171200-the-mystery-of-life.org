#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: The mystery of life

#+BEGIN_VERSE
When this poem started,
it needed an ocean of ink to finish it.
But I suppose one word is sufficient:

Be.

Which tells more --
  a lover's silent eyes,
  or her words?
#+END_VERSE
