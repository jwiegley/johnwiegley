#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Made to love the sun

#+BEGIN_VERSE
A flower is made
to love the sun;
by opening its arms
it discovers
the fulfillment of its being,
becoming radiant
and full of color
through that connection.
#+END_VERSE
