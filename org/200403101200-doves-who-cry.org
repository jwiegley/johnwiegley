---
title: Doves who cry
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
There are doves who cry
a mournful sadness
when day has fled.

They join the wolves
who bay
at the rising of the moon.

Each twilight
is lonely for its sun,
faded and gone
behind clouds of red;

I wonder at
nights like these:
how it all became
so somber, so soon.
#+END_VERSE
