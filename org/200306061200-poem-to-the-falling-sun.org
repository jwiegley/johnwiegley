#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Poem to the falling sun

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Of all the sky's blue,
its billows of uncarded wool,
its heart of gold
no cauldron holds
that rainbows guide us to;

Not one day can last,
nor all the heavens vast,
when night is come
and day is done
for blue and gold, alas.
#+END_VERSE
