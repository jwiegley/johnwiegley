---
title: Sleeping earth
category: Poems
location: 380 Esplanade Ave #211
---

    Tonight, only peace.
    The stars
    wheel about
    their constellations...
    the sea
    recounts
    a hushed lullaby...
    the moon
    winks
    at a sleeping earth...
    and a herd of
    silvered clouds
    pass by.
