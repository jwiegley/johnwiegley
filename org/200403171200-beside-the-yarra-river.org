title: Beside the Yarra river
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
On the grass
letting time slip away
like cotton from dandelion flowers...

Around me birds caw,
the grey clouds and water move past --
a raindrop finds my cheek.

Who am I, again?
#+END_VERSE
