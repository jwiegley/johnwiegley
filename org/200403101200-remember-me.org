title: Remember me
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Do you remember me?
The quiet eyes
watching you in dreams
writing love poems in the sand
whispering "come", too far away
for the words to reach your ears...

Do you remember me?
My heart was left alone
too long.
Now it's gone.
#+END_VERSE
