title: Honey
tags: poetry
edited: 04/25/2005
location: Horizon Park Center, Scottsdale, Arizona
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Her voice has a sweetness
like warm honey in my ear...
She soaks into me;
sugars my thoughts;
Drenches my heart
in amber warmth...
She fills me
with a golden light.

Now the bears are coming.

But however much
they take away,
she puts back
again.
#+END_VERSE
