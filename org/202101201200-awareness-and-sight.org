#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Awareness and sight

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Is the eye the gate of sight,
through which all light flows
and is not barred from entering,
though itself cannot be witnessed?

Is awareness like that sight?
#+END_VERSE
