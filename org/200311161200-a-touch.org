---
title: A touch
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Gently against the breeze --
a soft whisper --
her hand comes down upon my face.
With eyes like the incandescent sun,
burning heat straight into my root,
I find a connection there
that lasts until the morning's dawn.
#+END_VERSE
