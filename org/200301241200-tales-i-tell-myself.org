#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Tales I tell myself

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Too many words...
that fall on deaf ears;
too many sights...
that find the mind's eye blind.

So much to say,
were saying only enough;
but with each word
the distance grows too much.

I am not speaking of you:
These are the tales I tell myself.
#+END_VERSE
