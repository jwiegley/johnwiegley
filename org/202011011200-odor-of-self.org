---
title: The odor of self
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Only the self
takes note of
the odor of self.

To the real real
there's just
a mewling infant:

sometimes stinking
sometimes fresh;
always growing
always changing.
#+END_VERSE
