---
title: An Iranian beauty
tags: poetry
edited: 05/04/2005
location: 7373 N. 71st Place, Paradise Valley, AZ
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Her hair falls
like ebon silk
flowing to her sides;

Her fair skin
a honeyed milk
that calms my thirsty eyes;

Her sweet smile
undoes my heart,
time dallies in that stare;

I wonder still
those cherry lips...
Let every man beware.
#+END_VERSE
