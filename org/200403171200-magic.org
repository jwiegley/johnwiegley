title: Magic
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Magic requires that people allow it to happen.

If we close our mouths, how can the food enter?
If we close our ears, where is the sound?

It may seem like a silent world,
but this appearance is not the reality.
#+END_VERSE
