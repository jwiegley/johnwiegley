---
title: Someday
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
We always know that summer will come,
and the rainbow,
and happiness... someday.

But when is the important question.

Each day, are we standing at the front
of a thousand-day winter?
Or is spring just around the bend...
#+END_VERSE
