title: The waves
tags: poetry
edited: 03/07/2004
location: Sitges, Spain
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Perhaps a great pair of hands
seized the carpet of the sea,
shaking it with a might thrust,
leaving it to settle in its bed
with the breathe of the wind
rippling along.
#+END_VERSE
