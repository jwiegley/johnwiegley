#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: What changes you

#+BEGIN_VERSE
If it's the sun,
my chariot of wax is waiting.

If it's the moon,
I know a cow to take me there.

If it's the stars,
perhaps Hollywood will do.

If it's me,
I see much change in your future...
#+END_VERSE
