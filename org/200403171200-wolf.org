title: Wolf
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Hungry eyes, wolf;
you stare at me: your waiting meal.

I am as tasty inside
as the supple flesh around my bones.

It only takes knowing
how to gorge me open;
but I'm sure your razor teeth
are equal to that.
#+END_VERSE
