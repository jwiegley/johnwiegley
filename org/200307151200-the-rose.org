title: The rose
tags: poetry
edited: 03/22/2004
location: Italy
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The rose
over time
weeps its petals
in crimson drops of tears,
still forms
of velvet blood:
its last claim to beauty.
#+END_VERSE
