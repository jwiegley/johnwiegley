title: Carlton Park
tags: poetry

#+BEGIN_VERSE
The green army raises their swords,
gathering at the beachhead to launch
triumphantly into their final campaign!

Sorry, my mistake...
It was only the reeds by the lake,
viewed at a sideways angle, dreamily.
#+END_VERSE
