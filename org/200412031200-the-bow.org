#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: The bow

#+BEGIN_VERSE
I am a bow of strong wood
with forceful potential.
God has strung me
and knocked the arrow of my soul
pointed in His direction.
As I learn, I pull it back,
ever further, ever tighter.
Now it is taut:
to the breaking point.
All that is left for me
is to let go.
#+END_VERSE
