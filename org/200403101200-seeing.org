title: Seeing
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
This morning I awoke,
finding a canvas on my wall.

The image was stunning!
The artist, without peer.

I ran to my neighbor
and convinced him to look,

But he shrugged, saying,
"John, it's only a window."
#+END_VERSE
