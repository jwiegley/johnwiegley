#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Watsonville

#+BEGIN_VERSE
A cowboy buries hands in jean pockets,
a white gazebo shelters hopeful prayers.
The noon sun draws all eyes closed.

Ice cream melts more quickly
the younger the hand that holds it.
#+END_VERSE
