---
title: By the sandy shore
tags: poetry
written: 2 Lane Ave Apt 4, Caldwell, NJ
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
by the sandy shore
warmth of golden rays
hint of dreadful depths
lazily stretching waves

beyond, endless ocean
world beneath the world
folk of different temper
sand into pearl
#+END_VERSE
