#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: I plead

#+BEGIN_VERSE
I plead to your beauty: end my pain.
My skin is a door
and your fingers, each a key.
This house, wherein heaven dwells,
awaits your soft caress...
Awaken me.
#+END_VERSE
