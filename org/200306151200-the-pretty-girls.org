---
title: The pretty girls
tags: poetry
edited: 03/17/2004
location: Sitges, Spain
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Coffee-skinned, with chocolate eyes,
following hair, like bolts of silk;
Pendulum hips, that keep no time --
and teeth the white of richest milk.
#+END_VERSE
