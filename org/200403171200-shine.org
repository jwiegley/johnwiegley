---
title: Shine
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Be happy!
Be so happy
that clouds will lose their burdens,
the dawn comes eagerly,
and starlings give their melodies away.

And shine!
O ray of the Sun,
until glaciers melt, giving up their sadness
to the blue, blue waters,
where, now reunited, they can play.
#+END_VERSE
