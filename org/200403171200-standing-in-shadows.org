#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Standing in shadows

#+BEGIN_VERSE
I say: let them burn the Mona Lisa!
cast down the Parthenon!
and raze the pyramids of Egypt.

Any time man stands
in the shadow of what he creates,
we are bound for poverty.
#+END_VERSE
