title: Something I thought you said
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
You feel that you're past poetry;
that it's a sentimental thing -- for children.

Well, guess who I am,
and what I strive to be:
the ingenuousness of a child.
#+END_VERSE
