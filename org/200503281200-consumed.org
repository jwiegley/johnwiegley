title: Consumed
tags: poetry
edited: 04/19/2005
location: Tempe Bahá'í Center, Arizona
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
O fire, beautiful fire!
Your pain so intense
it tickles my soul.

I am burnt today,
my skin is black and
puffed and ruined.

Little by little
you turn me into
a delicate smoke;

Up, up, into the vast sky I go
floating on the finest breezes.
#+END_VERSE
