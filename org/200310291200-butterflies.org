#+filetags: :poetry:
#+title: Butterflies

#+BEGIN_VERSE
Why they make a nest of my stomach, I'll never know.
But they have such beautiful colors, such rhythms!
I forgive them.

Only let my caller call,
so that her voice may set them free.
#+END_VERSE
