---
title: Becoming
tags: poetry
---

#+BEGIN_VERSE
I kneel, bowing to the dust.
Giving up my life, I enter it;
Giving up my soul, I serve it;
Casting away my existence, I become it.

Now I am nothing, neither is it anything.
And thereby we become, both of us, His.
#+END_VERSE
